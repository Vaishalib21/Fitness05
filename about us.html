OUR STORY

In 1993, the first FitnessCentral opened in Bournemouth in the South Of England. Over the next decade and a half, Fitness first was present in over 16 countries, including India making it a leader in the global fitness industry. In 2018, FitnessCentral India was acquired by cure.fit, India's largest chain of fitness centres that offers energetic group workouts and multiple workout formats to choose from. We've become advocates for fitness, wellbeing and life enrichment. Our commitment to excellence is reflected not only in the quality equipment we provide but also in the lives we've improved. Our real success lies in the thousands of customers we've empowered to live longer, more joyful, and healthier lives. Join us in our mission to improve lives through fitness and wellness, because at FitnessCentral, your health is our passion.
